- `current_location()` → Your current location. You start in the living room of the house.
- `inventory()` → List of objects in your inventory. It starts empty.
- `go_to(place)` → Move to a location.
- `report_status()` → Say what you’re doing.
- `pick_up(item)` → Pick up an object by adding it to inventory.
- `give_to(item, recipient)` → Give an item form your inventory to someone.
